Welcome!

Hi there! I'm Rachel, a farm-loving mother of three with a passion for sewing! I have been sewing for over ten years and upon moving our two horses home to our little 'farmette', learned the hard way that our ponies are not easy on blankets! Whether they are alone or together, my girls always seem to manage to tear their blankets, lose strap, or downright get them filthy!

Rachel's Blankets started as a way to combine my love of farm life and sewing, all to benefit you! I know how expensive it is to buy new blankets and just how easily horses can destroy them. It's time we added a few more years to your favorite blankets to keep your equines dry, warm, and happy!
